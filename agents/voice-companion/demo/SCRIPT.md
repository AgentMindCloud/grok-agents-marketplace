# Demo Script — 30 seconds

**Format:** screen recording of an X voice DM conversation, captured in dark mode, with the real voice audio on top of a calm instrumental bed at −24 LUFS. No face cam. Brand-locked visuals throughout (pure `#0A0A0A`, `#00F0FF` accent only).

---

### 00:00 – 00:03 — Cold open

**Visual:** black screen. A single thin cyan waveform pulses once across the center, then resolves into the word **VOICE** in JetBrains Mono uppercase with a 1–2 px neon-glow edge.

**VO (narrator, separate track):**
> "A voice agent on X. Always on. Thirty-second demo."

### 00:03 – 00:10 — Turn 1

**Visual:** cut to an X DM thread between the user and `@YourVoiceCompanion`. A cyan "speak" indicator pulses on the user's side, then flips to the agent's.

**User (voice):** _"Hey, I'm thinking of writing something on multi-agent swarms. What angle did my last thread take?"_

**Agent (voice, under 3 seconds later):** _"You framed it as a contract between models — timeouts, budgets, audit log. Want to extend that?"_

**On-screen caption (sub):** `memory-keeper pulled 2 facts · 380 ms`

### 00:10 – 00:18 — Turn 2

**User (voice):** _"Yeah, but something shorter this time."_

**Agent (voice):** _"Then lead with the cost-ceiling idea. One beat, one thread."_

**On-screen caption:** `first-audio 410 ms · 1 sentence · 9 words`

### 00:18 – 00:25 — Turn 3, graceful handoff

**User (voice):** _"Okay write me the full outline."_

**Agent (voice):** _"Let me send that as a DM so you can read it."_

**Visual:** an X DM text bubble appears below the voice thread with a clean numbered outline. Cyan badge reads "handoff: voice → text".

### 00:25 – 00:30 — Close

**Visual:** the product lockup resolves at center.

```
VOICE COMPANION
Built for Grok on X.
```

Bottom-bar microcopy fades in:

```
GrokInstall is an independent community project.
Not affiliated with xAI, Grok, or X.
```

**VO:**
> "Voice Companion. Built with GrokInstall."

## Capture notes

- Record the X app at 1920×1080, 60 fps. Dark mode only.
- Use real voice audio (not TTS placeholder). The agent's voice is `grok-voice-2` / `ember` / `warm-conversational`.
- Keep the waveform motif subtle — it's the only on-screen animation besides the audio level indicator.
- Export: MP4 H.264 for X native, WebM VP9 for embed.
